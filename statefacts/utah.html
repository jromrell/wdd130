Fun Facts about Utah

The state of Utah is known as the beehive state and is one of 27 states that is landlocked. Utah is the youngest state in the country with 33% of it’s population being under 18 and the mean age being 31.3 years old. Only California and Alaska have more national parks than Alaska, and Utah is known for it’s skiing.

Utah was the 45th state in America. It is illegal to fish while on horseback and to hunt whales, which is especially intersting because Utah is landlocked. The Great Salt Lake is about four times saltier than any of the world’s oceans.Utah is the second-driest state in the United States after Nevada. On average, Utah has about 300 sunny days a year.Utah is home to the United States' first department store, Zions Co-operative Mercantile Institution. Today it is known as ZCMI
