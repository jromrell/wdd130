Home page

Main Page Growing up I always enjoyed hearing and getting to know random facts about random things. With this website being StatFacts, it will satisfy that curiosity you have about whatever state you live in, or you could be traveling through. The first two states that this site has is Idaho and Utah. I picked Idaho and Utah to be the first two states because I have lived in Idaho for most of my life, and Utah would be the most visited state of mine. As you explore StateFacts you will learn all sorts of new things about all sorts of new places!

Images for the Home page

Idaho

IDAHO People will come to this site out of curiosity. Fun facts are always a cool and fun thing to know and they are always very entertaining. On the Idaho page, people would come here for if they are curious about facts about the state of Idaho. For instance, you probably knew Idaho was the number one producer of potatoes, but did you know that they are the number one producers of trout as well? The state of Idaho is known as the “Gem State” because you can find nearly 75 types of precious stones in our state. Idaho is known as the lentil capital of the world. Idaho is known for their potatoes so it probably won’t come to any surprise that the world’s largest potato is in Idaho as well. This page is to entertain visotors and educate them on the state of Idaho.

Images for the Idaho

Utah

UTAH The Utah page will contain fun and interesting facts about the state of Utah. The state of Utah is known as the beehive state and is one of 27 states that is landlocked. Utah is the youngest state in the country with 33% of it’s population being under 18 and the mean age being 31.3 years old. Only California and Alaska have more national parks than Alaska, and Utah is known for it’s skiing. People will come to this page if they were traveling to Utah or just wanting to know more about the wonderful state.
